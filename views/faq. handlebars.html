Frequently Asked Questions

  • chevron_rightHow do I add a pet?
    Once logged in, click on the "Add a pet" button on the user profile page. You can add as many pets as you'd like.
  • chevron_rightHow can I share my pet profile with others users?
    Navigate to the Settings tab on your dashboard and scroll to the Share With section. Input the email address of the user you would like to share it with, and the pet profile will be added to their list of pets.
  • chevron_rightWhat access do other users have to pets I have shared with them?
    Those with whom the profile is shared with have owner privileges to view and edit information.
  • chevron_rightHow can I delete a pet from my profile?
    To delete a pet profile, navigate to the Settings tab on your dashboard and click on the "REMOVE FROM MY PETS" button at the bottom of the page. The pet will remain available to other users even if it is removed from your own profile.
  • chevron_rightWhat does the message board do?
    The message board allows users to post messages, notes and updates about your pet. It is a way to virtually interact with the other users who have access to your pet's profile.
