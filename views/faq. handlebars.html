Frequently Asked Questions

  • chevron_rightHow do I add a pet?
    Once logged in, click on the "Add a pet" button at the bottom of the user profile page. You can add as many pets as you'd like.
  • chevron_rightHow can I share my pet profile with others users?
    Navigate to the Settings tab on your dashboard and scroll to the Share With section. By typing in the email address of another user, caretaker or pet professional it will prompt the new user to sign up.
  • chevron_rightHow much can users I share my pet with see and do?
    Those with whom the profile is shared with have owner privileges to view and edit information.
  • chevron_rightHow can I delete my pet profile?
    To delete a pet profile, navigate to the Settings tab on your dashboard and click on the "REMOVE FROM MY PETS" button at the bottom of the page.
  • chevron_rightWhat does the message board do?
    The message board allows users to post messages, notes and updates about your pet. It is a way to virtually interact with the other users who have access to your pet's profile.
