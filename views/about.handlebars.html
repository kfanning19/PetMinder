About The PhiloPet Team!

  • chevron_rightWho we are
    We are three web developers in training. PhiloPet is a group project created during the last month of dev bootcamp. Combining our best front end and back end efforts we set out to build a tool for pet owners and lovers.
  • chevron_rightMission
    As pet owners ourselves, we know how challengeing it can be to keep up with an animal's needs. We built PhiloPet as a platform to organize and easily display information like diet and health. A key feature is the ability to share a pet's profile with the other humans in your animal's life such as the dog walker, the groomer, the veternarian or any member of the household.
